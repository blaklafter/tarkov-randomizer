Are you level 40+, attained your Kappa container, and looking for the Escape From Tarkov endgame? Sadly, there isn't one, at least not officially. I've been told there will be one when he game releases, which is slated for 3rd quarter 20Soon, likely on a Thursday. Until then, this will have to do

I created the original tarkov-randomizer to give players a way to generate randomized loadouts. Maybe you are tired of running meta M4's and hexgrid armors. Maybe you just like scav runs for the challenge they can provide because the loadouts are unpredictable. Maybe you are a streamer and need a way to kep it fresh for chat. Whatever your reason, I got you, bro.

This isn't really a comprehensive loadout generator, you've still got to decide on the type of ammunition you want to bring. And I didn't mess around with comtacs, because they are all kind of similar and it really just boils down to personal preference.

Whether you are a hardcore or casual gamer, rat or chad, I think you'll find some enjoyment in it.
